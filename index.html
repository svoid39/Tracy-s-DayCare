About Luzia Family Daycare

Our Philosophy

At Luzia Family Daycare, we believe that every child deserves a warm, nurturing environment where they feel safe, supported, and encouraged to explore the world around them.

As a family-owned childcare provider, we offer a home-like atmosphere that combines personalized attention with enriching learning experiences.

"As the owner of Luzia Family Daycare, my mission is to create a safe and loving environment where children can thrive. Every day, we strive to make a positive difference in the lives of the families we serve."

Nurturing Environment

We provide a home-like atmosphere where children feel safe, loved, and valued as individuals.

Holistic Development

Our program supports growth in all areas—social, emotional, cognitive, and physical.

Family Partnership

We work closely with parents to ensure each child's individual needs are met.

Our Approach

Play-Based Learning

We believe children learn best through play, exploration, and hands-on experiences.

Individualized Attention

Small group sizes ensure each child receives the attention they need.

Social-Emotional Growth

We focus on helping children develop kindness, empathy, and cooperation.

Parent Communication

Daily updates and regular conferences keep parents informed and involved.

Infant Program

(6 weeks - 18 months)

  • Nurturing, responsive care in a safe environment
  • Individualized feeding and sleeping schedules
  • Sensory experiences to support early development

Toddler Program

(18 months - 3 years)

  • Language-rich environment to support communication
  • Structured routines with plenty of play and exploration
  • Activities to develop fine and gross motor skills
